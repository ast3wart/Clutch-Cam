# ClutchCam
AI powered sports highlight generator. Just import your clips and we will create a highlight montage!

This is currently a WIP application, but once finished you could upload clips of any sport and our AI algorithm will automatically detect your highlights and will make short TikTok style clips of your moments. You can either download your clips or post them into our integrated social media.
